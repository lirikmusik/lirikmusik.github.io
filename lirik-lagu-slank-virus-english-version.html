7. ##Quiz #113 ##• english version• fair point• easy==========factorized : x³ - 11³ !​


~Algebra

__________

– Solution

= x³ - 11³

= x³ - (11 × 11 × 11)

= x³ - 1.331

= (x - 11)(x² + 11x + 121)

• Conclusion

So, the factorization of x³ - 11³ is (x - 11)(x² + 11x + 121)

• Answer Details

Subject: Mathematics

Class: VIII

Material: Algebra

Subject code: 2

Categorization code: 8.2.1
