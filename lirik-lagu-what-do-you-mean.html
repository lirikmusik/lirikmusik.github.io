6. What do you mean ? artinya apa ?


What do you mean dalam bahasa Indonesia artinya

Apa yang Anda maksud?Apa maksudnya?Apa artinya?

Kalimat ini biasa kita gunakan ketika kita tidak memahami akan sesuatu hal  (expressing lack of understanding).

Pembahasan

Ketika kita tidak memahami akan sesuatu hal, kita bisa menanyakan hal tersebut dengan phrase dibawah ini:

Sorry, I am not sure I understand.Sorry, I am not sure I know what you meanSorry, I don't quite follow you.What do you mean?What is that mean?I don't get itI 'm not sure I get your point

Untuk menanyakan kejelasan atau mengklarifikasi akan sesuatu hal dalam bahasa Inggris, kita bisa menggunakan phrase berikut ini:

Can you repeat, please?Can you clarify that for me?Could you be more spesific?What do you mean by that?Could you explain that, please?

Kata 'could' dipakai untuk situasi yang lebih formal.

35. Struktur dari 1) Do You Know What I Mean 2) What Do You Mean TOLONG DIJAWAB YA:))


The structure of:

Do You Know What I Mean?

DO ----> Auxiliary Verb

YOU ---> Subject

KNOW ---> Verb

WHAT ----> Interrogative Pronouns

I ----> subject

MEAN ----> Verb

?  ----> Question mark

.

What Do You Mean?

WHAT ----> Wh-questions

DO ----> Auxiliary Verb

YOU ---> Subject

MEAN ----> Verb

?  ----> Question mark

___________________________________

Yes/No Questions in the Present Simple

The question starts with Do or Does, and it will be answered with a yes or no.

The Pattern of Wh-Question in the Present Tense:

---->Auxiliary Verb + subject + verb 1

----> Do + you + want + to meet me?

___________________________________

Wh-Questions in the Present Simple :

The question starts with what, when, where, who, whom, which, whose, why and how.

We use them to ask for information. The answer cannot be yes or no because it's not a yes-no questions.

The Pattern of Wh-Question in the Present Tense:

---->Wh-word + Auxiliary Verb + Subject + Verb 1

---->When + do + you + want + to meet me?
