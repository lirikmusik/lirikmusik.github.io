12. In the parking lot there are 105 vehicles consisting of motorbikes and cars. If the total number of wheels of the entire vehicle (assumed without the spare tire) is 290 wheels, then the number of cars in the parking lot is …


Jawaban:

Ada 40 mobil di tempat parkir

English: There are 40 cars in the parking lot.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

misal motor x dan mobil y.

Maka:

x+y= 105

Kan mobil ada 4 roda dan motor 2 roda, maka:

2x+4y= 290

2(x+2y)= 290

x+2y= 145

x+2y= 145

x+y= 105

------------- -

y= 40

x+40= 105

x= 65

Jumlah mobil ada 40.
