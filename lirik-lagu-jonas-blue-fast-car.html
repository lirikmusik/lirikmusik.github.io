11. John said that no other car could go ______________ A. so fast like his car B. as fast like his car C. as fast like the car of him D. as fast as his car


Jawaban:

John said that no other car could go as fast as his car

D. as fast as his car

Penjelasan:

Kita memakai "as _______ as" (di tengah diisi dengan adjective/kata sifat atau adverb/kata keterangan) untuk membandingkan dua hal, dimana keduanya sama _____ nya.

Cantoh:

as high as (sama tingginya)

as quickly as (sama cepatnya)

as fast as (sama kencangnya)

as smart as (sama pintarnya)

Soal di atas mempunya arti:

John mengatakan tidak ada mobil lain yang sama kencangnya dengan mobilnya.

Contoh kalimat lain:

This ball is as big as a watermelon (bola ini sama besarnya dengan semangka)

My mother is as old as my father (ibuku sama tuanya dengan ayahlu)

I can do it as quickly as you do (saya bisa mengerjakannya secepat kamu)
