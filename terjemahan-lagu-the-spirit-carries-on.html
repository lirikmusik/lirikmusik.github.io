4. 1).The transport system in a flowering plant consists of xylem and phloem. With regards to both types of tissue selecy the correct statement from the following?A). Xylem carries food up from the roots and phloem carries minerals fown the plant.B). Xylem carries hormones up from the roots and phloem carries food down the plant.C). Xylem carries minerals down from the leaves and phloem carries food and hormones up thr plant.D). Xylem carries minerals up from the roots and phloem carries food and hormones down the plant.please bantuin kaka ini soal biologi tapi pake bahasa inggris???


A. Xylem carries food up from the roots and phloem carries minerals down the plant.

It is evident that xylem carries minerals from the root to the leaves and the phloem carries minerals all over the plant's body.

Xylem = carries water and mineral from the root to the leaves
Phloem = carries food (glucose, made by photosynthesis process) from the leaves to all over the plant's body.

21. Apa arti dari keep the spirit


artinya tetap semangat...Answer: 

The meaning of a phrase 'keep the spirit' means 'keep being motivated'. The word 'spirit' doesn't mean 'the soul which has been separated from the body' but 'the definitive qualities of one self.' In Indonesian, 'keep the spirit' can be translated as 'tetap semangat'. 
Here's the example in the sentence
- Keep the spirit for the next match as this is a life-and-death match for us.


Hopefully that suits your inquiry.
