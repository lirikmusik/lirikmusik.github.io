1.Cat

Cats or also known as the house cats have become one of the most popular humans companion since their domestication 9.500 years ago by the ancient Egypt. Most of them are furry. The common size of a cat is 25 cm in length and about 5 kg in weight. They are carnivorous mammal who love to hunt mouse, birds or lizard.
2.Rabbit

Rabbits are furry herbivorous mammals who have four legs, long ears, short tail and a divided upper lip. They can only be found in several parts of the world and most of them live in underground burrows. The common size of a rabbit is 20 to 50 cm with the average body weight of 2 kg. They spend most of their time sleeping in their dens for about 8 hours a day. Most of them can live up to 3 years in the wild.
